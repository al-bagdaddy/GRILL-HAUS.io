HAUS Ramly Burger

INGREDIENTS

    1 ready pack of beef patties
    6 slices of Cheddar cheese
    3pc burger bun
    3-6pc eggs
    60g shredded lettuce
    Prepare cooking as needed
    1 table spoon English Mustard
    1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
    1/2 table spoon of white pepper powder
    1/2 table spoon of Paprika
    40g of shredded onions
    Prepare barbecue sauce as needed
    Caramalized shallots

PREPARATION

Step1

Prepare the BBQ sauce in a bowl by coombining the mustard, mayo
White pepper, chilli powder toger and mix thm well.

Step2

CABBAGE: add 2 tablespoon of French onion dressing to the cabbage and mix well to set aside

Step3

SHALLOTS: add 2 tablespoons of butter/oil into a pan.
Add shallots, salt and sugar.
Fry them until golden-brown then remove and set aside.

Step4

BUNS: lightly toast burger buns on a hot pan and et aside

Step5

PATTIES: add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil in a flat pan until hot.
Add the patties and cook for 4-5 minutes per side
After you flip the patty, spread the special sauce on top. Be sure to cook patties all the way through the middle.
Lay cheese for each patty, then stack on on top of the other and set aside.
