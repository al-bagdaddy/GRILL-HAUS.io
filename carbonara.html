Spaghetti Carbonara

INGREDIENTS

    Salt
    2 large eggs & 2 large yolks
    room temperature
    1 ounce (1/3 packed cup) grated pecorino Romano
    Plus additional for serving
    1 ounce (1/3 cups) of grated Parmesan cheese
    Coarsely ground black pepper
    1 table spoon olive oil
    3.5 ounces of pancetta or bacon
    Sliced into pieces
    12 ounces of spaghetti

PREPARATION

Step 1:

Place a large pot of lightly salted water
No more than 1 table spoo of salt over high heat and set to boil.
Fill large bowl with hot water for serving and set aside

Step 2:

In mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, yolks
and pecorino and Permesan cheese. Season with a pinch of salt
and generous black pepper

Step 3:

Set water to boile. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add pork or pancetta and saute until the fat is rendered soft and golden.
Remove from heat and set aside while maintaining oil in skillet.

Step 4:

Add pasta to the water and boil until a bit firm. Just before pasta is ready,
reheat bacon or pancetta in skillet if needed. Reserve 1 cup of
pasta water, then drain pasta and add to the skillet over low heat.
Stir for about a minute

Step 5:

Empty serving bowl of hot water. Dry it and add
hot pasta mixture. stir in cheese mixture,
adding some reserved pasta water if needed for creaminess.
Serve immidiately, dressing it with a bit of additional grated
pecorino and pepper
